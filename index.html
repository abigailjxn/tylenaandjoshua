The Couple

Tylena

The legend goes that Saint Valentine begged sweet baby Tylena to wait one more day to come into the world, because he was so afraid his day would be overshadowed by her. Being the kind soul that she is, Ty obliged and came into the world on February 15th, 1992. Born in Kansas City and raised in Wichita, this sweet midwestern girl traded in wheat stalks for skyscrapers after graduating from the University of Kansas (Rock Chalk) and making her big move up to the Windy City! She's both down to earth and classy as they come. Her laugh can warm any heart and her beauty will make you blush. Ty majored in speech pathology, loves kids, and got involved with the Navigators during her freshman year of college. Little did she know back then that her ties to this campus ministry would lead her to meet a boy...

Joshua

Joshua Austin Jeans was born on August 14th, 1992 to the wonderful Tony and Kim Jeans. He is loving referred to as a “Gentle Giant”, (He been 6’2 since the 7th grade, but he’s all heart) He has a younger brother named Jake and they grew up Kennesaw, GA. Josh went to Vanderbilt in Nashville for college and majored in Human and Organizational Development. My man was INVOLVED at Vandy. He was in BYX fraternity where he met most of his lifelong buddies, and also was heavily involved in Navs at Vandy. After graduating, Josh stayed in Nashville to go on staff with the Navigators, which eventually led us to meet each other. He is the best person I know. He’s kind, thoughtful, loving, handsome, generous, and hilarious. I cannot wait to be his wife!

Why Nashville?

Great question! We’re glad that you asked. Nashville has played a big part in our relationship. It’s where we had our first kiss, our biggest fight, and it’s where we decided that we would take the risk on each other and become boyfriend and girlfriend while living 478 miles apart. We’ve been to concerts and parties here, cooked meals and gone on long walks, explored and rested. It is a city that we both love and it has loved us back.

And practically speaking, we did it for you guys! With our main friend and family bases being in Kansas, Chicago, Nashville, and Georgia, Nashville stood out as the most central option. Our hope was to help our friends and family get to our wedding! Nashville is within driving distance for more of our loved ones than any other option we looked into. Hopefully if you made I this far, you will make it to the 615 to celebrate with us! That’s right- Nashville’s area code and our wedding date are one-in-the-same!
